Motorcross


A skilled motocross and desert rally rider, Mohammed Balooshi was the first Emirati to take part in the legendary Dakar Rally..

A short biography and some facts about Mohammed:
  • Mohammed Balooshi always knew he was destined for something great.
  • It took him three years of intensive practice to be able to enter his first competition.
  • Balooshi coached seven young riders to drive for the first time in a desert
  • In 2012, he became the first Emirati to participate in the Dakar Rally
  • Balooshi continued to excel in races across the Middle East and in 2018 won his most prestigious title to date when he claimed victory at the FIM Baja World Cup
